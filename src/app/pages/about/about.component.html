Hello! I'm a web developer primarily focused on UI / UX development.

My story.

My name is George Gordon VI but to make easier just call me Eddie. I was born and raised in Southern California. I graduated from the University of South Carolina with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems and Business Information Management. I have been extremely fortunate to travel and live on both sides of the country. I have a love for sports, especially if you mention beach volleyball.

My passion.

I am immensely passionate about every aspect of web development. I have always had the “let’s see if I can do it” mentality. I started publishing content online when I was 14 years old. From portfolio websites built in simple HTML and CSS, to websites built with server-side logic like PHP. Then moving on to more structured frameworks and libraries like Angular and React, along with using more flexible server-side frameworks like Express JS. The entire process from concept, to design, to functionality and usability, and eventually deployment absolutely intrigues me. I am a front-end web developer with full stack knowledge.
